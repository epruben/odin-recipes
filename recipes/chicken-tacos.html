Ingredients

  • Rotisserie Chicken
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Tortillas
  • Bell Pepper
  • Jalapeno
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Shred the rotisserie chicken
  2. Slice the onion, jalapeno, and bell pepper
  3. Grill the vegetables for 6 minutes on the range
  4. Season the vegetables with salt and pepper
  5. Add the shredded chicken to the pan and cook for 10 minutes
  6. Squeeze lime into the pan 1 minutes before finishing the cook
  7. Heat tortillas on a small pan at low heat
  8. Dice cilatro
  9. Assemble tacos and serve
  10. Enjoy!
