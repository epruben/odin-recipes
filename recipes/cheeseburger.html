Ingredients

  • Potato Buns
  • Ground Beef 80% lean
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Chicken Bullion
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Measure 2 tablespoons of salt, pepper, cumin, garlic, onion powder, and chicken buillon
  2. Mix the spices into the ground beef
  3. Form the beef into 4 2oz patties
  4. Grill the patties on medium heat for 5-6 minutes on each side
  5. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty
  6. Let the cheese melt for 1-2 minutes
  7. Toast the potato buns on the grill for 1-2 minutes
  8. Assemble the cheeseburger
  9. Enjoy your cheeseburger!
