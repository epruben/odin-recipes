Ingredients

  • Whole Grain Bread
  • Jif Natural Creamy Peanut Butter
  • Smucker's Blackberry Jam

Steps

  1. Lightly toast 2 slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter over 1 slice of bread
  3. Spread jam over the other slice
  4. Combine the two slices
  5. Enjoy!
